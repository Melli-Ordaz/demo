特征

DeepKE 是一个支持低资源、长篇章的知识抽取工具，可以基于PyTorch实现命名实体识别、关系抽取和属性抽取功能。

多任务

DeepKE包括了三个模块，可以进行命名实体识别、关系抽取以及属性抽取任务。

多场景

DeepKE各个模块下包括各自的子模块。其中关系抽取模块就有常规模块、文档级抽取模块以及低资源少样本模块。

多使用方式

您可以通过pip直接使用，也可以修改代码以供“python setup.py instll”使用。

这些任务启发我们是否去创造一个通用的框架。

基于此，我们将分词和句子都视为通用知识，并设计了一个简单的统一模型。我们的原型模型研究了用单一体系结构弥合任务之间差距的可能性，并为统一自然语言理解提供了未来的见解。此外，由于缺乏实用且稳定的工具包来支持这些任务的实施、部署和评估，所以我们开发了DeepKE，该工具包是对现有工具集的补充，如Spacy2用于命名实体识别，TagMe用于实体链接，OpenKE用于知识嵌入，斯坦福OpenIE用于开放信息提取，OpenNER用于关系提取。

开发者说

Natural language processing covers a wide variety of tasks with token-level or sentencelevel understandings. We provide a simple insight that most tasks can be represented in a single universal extraction format. To be specific, we introduce a prototype model and provide an open-source and extensible toolkit called DeepKE for various extraction tasks.

张宁豫

AZFT 研究员

The essence of a knowledge graph is to use the graph's structure to describe the relationship between things and precipitate domain knowledge. It is an important cornerstone to realize cognitive intelligence. It has been widely used in many fields, such as search engines, intelligent question and answer, language semantic understanding, extensive data decision analysis, etc.

陈华钧

浙江大学教授

DeepKE allows developers to train custom models to extract information from the text and supports quick model validation for researchers. I think this work can bring convenience and inspiration to industry and academia.
